What is the Dark Day?

The day will go dark at noon. This will be the result of America enforcing a religious law that is contrary to God's law. God will reveal His displeasure by allowing seven judgments to fall upon this earth.

1

Dark Day

The sun goes down at noon. The earth is darkened in the clear day, exactly as Amos foretold.

2

Earthquake

The ground itself answers the warning as the second judgment falls.

3

Tsunami in Tampa, FL

The waters rise against the coast of Florida.

4

Asteroid in the Gulf

An asteroid strikes the Gulf of America.

5

War

China and Russia invade America.

6

The White House Destroyed

The seat of the nation's power falls.

7

A Statue Like Lot's Wife

The final judgment, echoing the fate written in Genesis.
