About Saving Health Ministries

Saving Health Ministries was founded on a single conviction: that the warnings written in scripture are written for our day, and that people deserve to hear them plainly before the dark day comes.

Through video teaching rooted in the King James Bible, this ministry walks through prophecy verse by verse — what is coming, why it is coming, and what God asks of those who are watching. Nothing here is softened, and nothing is added. The message is the message.

Every teaching is freely available. If this work speaks to you, the best thing you can do is watch, share it with someone who needs to hear it, and prepare.
